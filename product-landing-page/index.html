The magical potion KUMKUMADI OIL which is also used as a moisturizer is suitable for almost every skin type and best works for dry, flaky skin. The oil is a wonderful blend of plant, flower, fruit, and milk extracts which makes it the magical oil for pure skin.

  • Helps over pigmentation and dark spots.
  • Work best for skin brightening and lighting.
  • Tone your skin by providing the correct cell formation.
  • Can be used by anyone, of any skin type.

How to Use

Step: 1 Wash face, pat dry.

Step: 2 Gently massage 2-3 drops of Vasu Kumkumadi Tailam over face and neck till it gets absorbed.

Step: 3 For best results, allow the application to remain overnight.
